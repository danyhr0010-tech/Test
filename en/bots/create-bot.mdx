<br />

Follow these steps to create a bot in AI Studio from the web interface.

## From the web interface

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access the Bots page">
    Log into AI Studio. The main page shows the Bots section with the sidebar and the editing canvas.

    <Frame caption="AI Studio - Bots page">
      <img src="/images/bots-page.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open the creation form">
    Click **"New bot"** in the left sidebar.

    <Frame caption="Create a new bot modal">
      <img src="/images/create-bot-modal.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fill in the fields">
    | Field | Description |
    |---|---|
    | **Bot Name** | Unique name to identify your bot |
    | **Bot Type** | `voicebot`, `voiceagent`, `chatbot`, or `apibot` |

    <Frame caption="Selecting a bot type">
      <img src="/images/bot-type-select.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create">
    Click **"Create"**. The bot is created with version `00001` and tag `dev`.
  </Step>
</Steps>
