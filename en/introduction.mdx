AI Studio is a conversational AI platform that allows you to create, configure, and deploy voice bots and chatbots. With AI Studio you can design visual conversation flows, integrate language models (LLM), voice engines (TTS/STT), and connect your bots to telephony channels.

## What you can do with AI Studio

  <Card title="Create Bots" icon="robot" href="/en/quickstart">

  </Card>
  <Card title="Conversation Flows" icon="diagram-project" href="/en/bots/conversation-flow">

  </Card>
  <Card title="AI Integration" icon="brain" href="/en/bots/voice-configuration">
